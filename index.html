THE GIST

I'm Emma Grdina, a Senior Product Designer at The Washington Post. A passionate communicator, I enjoy getting to the root of problems and proposing design solutions both big and small.

The Coral Project

The Coral Project is a partnership among The New York Times, The Washington Post and the Mozilla Foundation funded by the Knight Foundation. As the Lead Designer, I oversee the visual design and overall user experience.

The Feminism Project

The Feminism Project sougth to answer what feminism meant in 2015. I created a one-of-a kind commenting experience built on the theory that if you lead with a question, a focused discussion will follow.

The Washington Post site refresh

The Washington Post spent more than one year reconfiguring article pages and section fronts followed by a homepage refresh with the intention of optimizing the site's experience for each of our users no matter where they engage with our content.
