The Coral Project

TLDR

The Coral Project is a collaboration among The New York Times, The Washington Post and the Mozilla Foundation, funded by the Knight Foundation creating better tools for commenters and journalists to engage with each other. As the Lead Designer, I oversee the visual design, information architecture and overall user experience.
